Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Tree removal in Atlanta typically ranges from $400 to $2,500+ depending on the size of the tree, its location, and how difficult the access is. A small tree near a driveway is on the lower end; a large oak near power lines or a structure will be higher. We provide free on-site estimates so you know exactly what to expect.
In the City of Atlanta, you generally need a permit to remove any tree with a diameter of 6 inches or more (measured at chest height). Some areas like Sandy Springs and Brookhaven have their own tree ordinances. We can help you navigate the permit process as part of our service.
Stump grinding uses a machine to chew the stump down 6-12 inches below the soil surface. It's faster and more affordable. Stump removal extracts the entire root ball, which leaves a larger hole but is necessary if you're building or planting in that exact spot. We can advise which option is best for your situation.
Most single-tree removals are completed in 2-5 hours, including cleanup. Larger trees, trees near structures, or jobs requiring crane work can take a full day. Multi-tree or lot clearing jobs may take 2-3 days. We'll give you a time estimate along with the price.
Yes. Es Tree Service is fully insured with general liability coverage. This protects your property in the unlikely event something goes wrong during the job. We're happy to provide proof of insurance before we start work.
By default, we haul away all wood and debris. If you'd like to keep the firewood or larger logs, just let us know and we'll cut them to length and stack them where you'd like. There's no extra charge for that.
