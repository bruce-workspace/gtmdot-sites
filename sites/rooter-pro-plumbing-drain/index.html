What We Do

Full-Service Residential & Commercial Plumbing

From a dripping faucet to a full repipe, Megan's team handles it all. Licensed, insured, and backed by a $10,000 TrustDALE guarantee on every job.

Drain Cleaning & Rooter

Clogged drains, slow sinks, main line backups. Rooting, hydrojetting, and camera inspections to get the real picture.

Sewer Line Repair

Sewer line repair, replacement, and trenchless options. Camera diagnostics to find the break, fix the cause, prevent the repeat.

Water Heaters

Tank and tankless installation, repair, and replacement. Energy-efficient upgrades available. Emergency water heater service 24/7.

Leak Detection

Slab leaks, hidden leaks, and mystery water damage. We locate the source without tearing up your walls.

Repipes & Water Lines

Full home repipes, water line repair and replacement, gas line service. PEX, copper, and CPVC systems.

Fixtures & Faucets

Toilet, sink, and fixture installation and repair. Garbage disposals, sump pumps, ejection pumps, and shower valves.

24/7 Emergency

Burst pipe at midnight? Water heater flooding? We dispatch around the clock. Emergency service available every day.

Commercial Plumbing

Commercial maintenance contracts, tenant improvements, and large-scale plumbing projects. Residential and commercial under one roof.

About Megan

A Family Plumbing Shop with Real Guarantees

Woman-Owned, Family-Run

Megan Dammann founded Rooter Pro Plumbing GA in May 2021. She's one of the rare female owner-operators in Atlanta's plumbing trade.
