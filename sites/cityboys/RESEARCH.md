## Business Story

Curtis A. Jones Jr. founded City Boys R Us in October 2009, starting small with a Craigslist ad. He holds an Electronics Technology Diploma from Atlanta Technical College. Over 16+ years, he built the company from a one-man startup into one of the most reviewed appliance repair services in Metro Atlanta, accumulating nearly 400 Google reviews. The company became an LG Authorized Servicer in 2021. Curtis still works as a hands-on repair technician in addition to running the business. Kimberly Brown serves as Manager. The business operates 24/7 with emergency service availability and has maintained an A+ BBB rating (though not BBB accredited).
