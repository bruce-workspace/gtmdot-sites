About Curtis

From a Craigslist Ad to 391 Reviews

Built From Scratch

In 2009, Curtis Jones posted a small ad on Craigslist offering appliance repair in Atlanta. He had an Electronics Technology Diploma from Atlanta Technical College, a toolbox, and the belief that if you fix things right and treat people well, word spreads.

Sixteen years later, City Boys R Us is one of Metro Atlanta's most-reviewed appliance repair services. Curtis and his team cover 6 counties, service 16+ brands including premium names like Bertazzoni, Thermador, and Bosch, and take calls 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

In 2021, City Boys R Us became an LG Authorized Servicer, giving them access to genuine LG parts and factory training. The A+ BBB rating tells the same story the 391 Google reviews tell: Curtis does the work right, charges fairly, and explains what he's doing so you understand your appliance when he leaves.
