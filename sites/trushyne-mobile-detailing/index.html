About Demetric

Owner-Operator Since 2018

The Detailer Who Shows Up

Demetric R. Johnson founded Trushyne Mobile Detailing LLC in June 2018. Seven years in, customers still mention him by name in reviews because he still shows up and does the work himself. That's why the standard stays consistent across hundreds of vehicles.
