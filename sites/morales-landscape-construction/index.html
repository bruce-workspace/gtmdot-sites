What We Do

Landscape, Hardscape, Site Prep

One crew covering the work most homeowners split across three contractors — design, build, grade, plant, water, and haul it away after.

Landscape Design

Full yard design including plantings, beds, trees, turf, and layout. We scope honestly — you'll hear what will work in Atlanta soil and what won't.

Hardscape + Patios

Paver patios, retaining walls, walkways, fire pits, steps. Proper base prep, clean edge work, and lines that still look right in five years.

Sod + Grading

Land leveling, soil amendments, sod install. We've leveled yards for backyard kid pools and regraded after construction for proper drainage.

Irrigation

Sprinkler system design, install, repair. Drip lines for beds, rotor heads for lawn, smart controllers. Water saved is money saved.

Tree Work + Stumps

Tree trimming, removal, planting, and stump grinding. The trees that are overgrown, storm-damaged, or in the wrong place — we handle.

Excavation + Junk Removal

Site prep, debris haul-off, old-landscape rip-out, post-construction cleanup. Start fresh without coordinating a separate hauler.

Water Features

Ponds, fountains, small water-feature install and repair. The accent that elevates a backyard from yard to garden.

Artificial Turf

Synthetic turf install for high-traffic areas, pet yards, and shaded lawns where grass won't grow. Proper base, permeable, long-wearing.

Our Story

Jose Morales, Owner-Operator

Six Years. One Crew. One Phone.

Jose Morales founded Morales Landscape & Construction in 2019 with a simple idea: one crew, full scope. Not a design firm that subcontracts install. Not a lawn company that won't touch hardscape. One five-person team that handles the design, the grading, the planting, the stones, the trees, and the cleanup.

Six years later, that model hasn't changed. Jose does estimates personally. The customer reviews mention him by name because he's the one doing the walk-through, answering the questions, and often swinging the shovel. It's why 34 Thumbtack customers have hired him across full-yard transformations, hardscape-only projects, and everything in between.

Single-source landscape and construction means fewer coordination failures. One invoice, one warranty, one person you call when something needs attention.
