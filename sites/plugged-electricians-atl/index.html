About Sherea

The Female Electrician

Breaking the Mold Since 2018

Sherea Jones didn't just enter a male-dominated trade. She built a company in it. Plugged Electricians ATL was founded in 2018 with a team of five, a commitment to NEC code compliance, and a belief that Atlanta families deserve affordable electrical work from people who care about the outcome.

With over a decade of personal experience in the electrical field, Sherea has grown Plugged Electricians into a full-service residential and commercial operation. Her team handles everything from basic outlet repairs to EV charger installations (ChargePoint and Leviton certified) and smart home wiring.

Every technician on her team is background checked, licensed, and insured. The company follows NEC code and NFPA safety protocols on every job. And yes, she offers free electrical panel inspections, because catching a hazard early is worth more than billing for one later.
