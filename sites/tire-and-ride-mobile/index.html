What We Do

Tires, Roadside & More

From a single flat fix on I-285 at 3 AM to a full set of new tires in your driveway, Tire & Ride handles it all, around the clock.

Mobile Tire Install

New and used tires installed at your location. All-inclusive mobile install pricing. We bring the tires and equipment to you.

Flat Tire Repair

Plug and patch repair done on-site. No towing needed for most flats. Back on the road fast.

24/7 Roadside

Jump-starts, lockouts, fuel delivery, and tire changes. Any hour, any day. Call the dispatch line and help is on the way.

Balancing & Alignment

Computerized wheel balancing, tire rotation, TPMS reset, and wheel alignment to keep your ride smooth and safe.

Towing

When mobile repair isn't enough, we tow. Flatbed and traditional towing available across Metro Atlanta.

New & Used Tires

Full inventory of new tires from major brands and quality used tires. Competitive pricing on every size and every vehicle type.

About Us

Atlanta's Around-the-Clock Tire Team

Two Locations. One Mission.

Tire & Ride Mobile operates from two Atlanta locations and serves the entire metro area, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. What started as a mobile tire service has grown into a full roadside operation with a team that includes Will, Josh, Marlo, and more.

With 573+ Google reviews and an A+ BBB rating, the numbers tell the story: when Atlanta drivers are stranded, this is who they call. The team handles everything from a quick flat patch on the highway to a full set of new tires installed at your home, plus jump-starts, lockouts, fuel delivery, and towing.

Their pricing is transparent and competitive. Their hours are always. And every technician who shows up is professional, fast, and focused on getting you back on the road.
