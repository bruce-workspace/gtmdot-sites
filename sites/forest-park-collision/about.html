About Forest Park Collision

Forest Park Collision has been quietly fixing Atlanta cars on Central Avenue for years, building a 70+ five-star reputation almost entirely on word-of-mouth.

Kevin and his team handle the full range of collision and body work — fender benders, sideswipes, full-quarter-panel rebuilds, paint matching, dent removal, and insurance-claim work. They don't subcontract paint or body work; everything happens in-house, which is why customers consistently say the cars come back looking 'brand new.'

If you've never been through an accident before, the process can feel intimidating. Kevin walks you through it — what your insurance covers, what a fair estimate looks like, what the timeline actually is. No surprises, no upsells, no pressure.

Service area

We serve Forest Park, College Park, East Point, Hapeville, South Atlanta, Riverdale, Morrow, Jonesboro. If you're within 20 miles south of downtown Atlanta of Forest Park, we can typically be on-site same week.

Meet Kevin and the Forest Park Collision team
