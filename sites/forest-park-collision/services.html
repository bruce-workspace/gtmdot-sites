Services we offer

Forest Park Collision handles every step of body and collision work in-house — no subcontracting paint or panel work to outside shops. Free estimates, fair pricing, and insurance work welcome.

Collision Repair

Fender benders to full-panel rebuilds. We handle insurance claims, order the parts, and deliver a car you can't tell was wrecked.

Auto Body Paint

Color matching that disappears into the rest of the car. Full repaints, panel blends, fresh clear coats. Done in our own paint booth.

Dent & Bumper Repair

Door dings, parking-lot dents, scuffed bumpers, cracked covers. Quick turnaround on small jobs, often same-week.

Insurance Claim Help

We handle the adjuster, the supplements, the photos, the rental coordination. You sign the work order — we deal with the paperwork.
