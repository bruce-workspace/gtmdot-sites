Our Approach

Why One Crew Matters

Most homeowners end up juggling three contractors for a single project — a kitchen remodel needs a cabinet guy, a tile guy, and a paint guy. Schedules slip, hand-offs break down, and each vendor points at the next when something's off.

TGP does the whole thing. Kitchen through punch-list, one crew, one project manager, one invoice. It's why neighbors along the Chamblee and Brookhaven corridors refer us for both the big projects and the Saturday punch-list.

The "Home Services" in the name isn't a marketing phrase — it's the operating model. Remodel scope plus handyman scope, available on the same call.
