What's Included

Detail & Ceramic, Start to Finish

From the hand wash to the ceramic cure — the work that separates a premium detail from a drive-through carwash.

Ceramic Coating

Multi-year paint protection. Hydrophobic, UV-resistant, slick to the touch. Correct prep and cure matters — we don't cut corners on either.

Hand Wash + Wax

Two-bucket wash method, iron decontamination, hand-applied wax. The standard drive-through carwash finish doesn't come close.

Interior Detail

Full vacuum, seat + carpet shampoo, leather conditioning, trim restoration. The work that makes the cabin feel new again.

Exterior Paint Polish

Paint correction, swirl removal, clay bar prep. The surface prep that makes ceramic coating actually deliver on its promise.

Headlight Restoration

Hazy, yellowed, or oxidized headlights restored to clear optics. Better night visibility, cleaner front-end look.

Engine Bay Detail

Safe, thorough engine bay cleaning — covers sensitive components, degreases, dresses plastic and rubber. Makes the engine bay look as good as the paint.
