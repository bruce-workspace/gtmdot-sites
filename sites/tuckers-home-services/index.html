What We Do

Gutter Specialists & Exterior Care

Gutters are our bread and butter, but we handle everything on the outside of your home that keeps water, weather, and wildlife where they belong.

Gutter Cleaning

Thorough debris removal, flushing, and inspection. Maintenance plans available for automatic service 2-3 times per year so you never worry about clogs.

Gutter Repair

Sagging sections, leaking joints, loose hangers, and damaged downspouts. Shaun fixes what's broken and tells you honestly if replacement makes more sense.

Gutter Guards

Screen and guard installation to reduce debris buildup and extend the time between cleanings. Right product for your tree coverage and roof type.

Gutter Installation

Seamless metal gutters sized and pitched correctly for your roof. Shaun once told a customer their gutters were the wrong size before quoting new ones. That's the kind of honesty you get.

Fascia & Soffit Repair

Rotted fascia boards and damaged soffits replaced before they lead to bigger problems. We catch wood rot early and fix it right.

Siding & Chimney

Siding repair and installation, chimney pans, shrouds, and exterior repairs. Full exterior coverage so you only need one call.

Pressure Washing

Driveways, walkways, siding, and decks cleaned to remove years of buildup. Often bundled with gutter cleaning for a full exterior refresh.

Maintenance Plans

Set it and forget it. Tucker's comes out 2-3 times per year to clean and inspect your gutters automatically. No scheduling, no reminders needed.

Our Story

A Family Protecting Families

Shaun & Misty Tucker

Tucker's Home Services is a husband-and-wife operation. Shaun leads the crews in the field. Misty manages scheduling and makes sure every appointment runs on time. Together, they've built their livelihoods on helping people maintain their homes.

They started in 2005 with a simple mission: do honest gutter work at a fair price. Twenty-one years later, that mission hasn't changed. What has changed is the team (now roughly 10 employees) and the range of services, which grew because customers kept asking "can you also fix my fascia?" and "what about my siding?" The answer was always yes.

Their Nextdoor reputation (142 reviews at 5.0 stars) tells the story better than any marketing. Neighbors recommend Shaun to neighbors, and those neighbors recommend him to more. That's how you build a business that lasts two decades.

Gutter Service FAQ

Most homes in Metro Atlanta need gutter cleaning 2 to 3 times per year, depending on tree coverage. Heavy pine or oak coverage may need quarterly attention. Our maintenance plans handle this automatically so you never have to think about it.
Yes. Tucker's offers recurring maintenance plans where we come out 2-3 times per year automatically to clean and inspect your gutters. You don't have to remember to schedule or call. We handle it, and you get consistent, reliable care for your home's drainage system.
Shaun will give you an honest assessment. If a section can be repaired, he'll repair it. If your gutters are the wrong size for your roof (which he's caught before), he'll tell you that too instead of just patching a system that won't work properly. Multiple reviewers specifically mention that Tucker's does not upsell or push unnecessary services.
Rotted fascia often starts as soft or discolored wood behind your gutters. You might notice paint peeling, gutters pulling away from the house, or visible dark spots along the roofline. Left untreated, it spreads to the soffit and roof decking and gets much more expensive to fix. We catch it early during gutter cleanings and repair it before it becomes a major project.
Yes. If a gutter section falls, a downspout detaches during a storm, or water is pouring over and threatening your foundation, call us. We offer emergency services and will prioritize getting to you quickly. Shaun's Thumbtack response time averages around 4 hours.
