## Company Story
Tucker's Home Services is a family-owned company founded by Shaun and Misty Tucker in 2005. They have been dedicated to providing gutter cleaning, installation, and repair services to homeowners across the Metro Atlanta area for over 20 years. Shaun handles the field work and leads crews, while Misty manages scheduling and appointments. They describe themselves as having "built their livelihoods on helping people maintain their homes" and take pride in honesty, hard work, and reliability. The company has grown from a small family operation to a team of approximately 10 employees. They have handled "everything from the simple to the lavish." They offer maintenance plans where they come out automatically two or three times a year for gutter care. They also offer emergency services and free project estimates.
