What We Fix

Mobile Auto & Diesel Repair

From a check engine light on your daily driver to a DOT inspection on your fleet truck, Celena brings shop-level repair to wherever your vehicle is.

Mobile Diagnostics

Full engine diagnostics for gas and diesel. Check engine light, no-start, noise, vibration, and TPMS issues. Accurate diagnosis before any wrench turns.

Oil Changes & Tune-Ups

Preventive maintenance at your home or business. Oil changes, filter replacements, fluid checks, and scheduled service to keep your vehicle running right.

Brake Service

Hydraulic and air brake systems. Pads, rotors, calipers, lines, and complete brake diagnostics for cars, trucks, and commercial vehicles.

Electrical & Alternator

Battery replacement, alternator repair, electrical diagnostics, headlight repair, and power steering. If it's electrical, Celena can trace it.

Coolant & Engine Repair

Diesel & Commercial

Fleet Service

About Celena

The Mechanic Who Shows Up

ASE Certified. 15+ Years. 1,000+ Customers.

Celena started Tech On The Way as a pilot program in New Jersey in 2016. By 2019 she had proven the model: bring dealership-level diagnostics and repair directly to the customer, wherever their vehicle is. In 2021, she brought it to Atlanta.

Since then, she has served over 1,000 customers across 10 Metro Atlanta counties. Her ASE certification is earned, not bought. Every technician on the team meets ASE, OSHA, DOT, and manufacturer standards. Every repair comes with a 12-month, 12,000-mile warranty.

What makes Celena different isn't just that she comes to you. It's that she explains what she's finding as she works, so you understand your vehicle. Multiple reviewers note her thoroughness, her patience, and the fact that she never upsells. One customer said she rescued them from a breakdown 550 miles from home on a weekend evening when no shops were open.
