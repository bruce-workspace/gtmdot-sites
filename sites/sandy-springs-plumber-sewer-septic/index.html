Why This Matters

Septic Is the Moat

North Fulton has older homes on septic. Most plumbers don't touch them.

When your septic field backs up, the typical "24/7 plumber" calls a septic specialist on your behalf — adding a referral fee, a day of coordination, and a crew that doesn't know your line. We do both sides of the wall. Sewer line repair, septic tank pump, field issue, drain field restoration — same crew that fixes the kitchen sink.

What We Do

Plumbing, Sewer, Septic

Full residential plumbing plus the outdoor and underground work most North Fulton shops refer out.

Septic Tank Service

Tank pumping, lid access repair, baffle replacement, inspection. Scheduled maintenance and emergency response.

Sewer Line Repair

Cleanout, camera scope, hydro-jet, trenchless repair when possible. Root intrusion, collapsed line, clay-pipe failure — the corridor-age issues.

Septic Field Work

Drain field assessment, distribution-box repair, lateral line work. Problems that need soil knowledge as much as plumbing knowledge.

Drain Cleaning

Kitchen, bath, laundry, main line. Snake, hydro-jet, camera diagnostic to find the cause, not just clear the clog.

Water Heaters

Tank and tankless — repair, replace, service. Gas or electric. Install cleanup included.

Leak Detection

Slab leaks, wall leaks, yard-line leaks. Precise location so repair is surgical, not exploratory.

Pipe Repair + Replace

Supply and drain pipe repair and replacement. Copper, PEX, PVC, cast iron — whatever your house has.

Fixtures + Toilets

Toilet replace, faucet install, garbage disposal swap, shower valve repair. The everyday plumbing.
