What We Do

Mobile Tire & Wheel Service

AJ saw the need for a complete tire service away from the shop. So he built one that comes to your home, office, or wherever you are on the road.

Tire Installation

1 to 4 tires installed at your location. New tires from economy to major brands at wholesale pricing. Sizes 13 inches through 30 inches for cars, trucks, SUVs, and trailers.

Flat Tire Repair

Puncture repair done right at your location. No towing needed. AJ carries the equipment to fix most flats on the spot so you can get back on the road.

Wheel Balancing & TPMS

Computerized wheel balancing and tire pressure monitoring system reset. Smooth ride, no vibrations, warning lights cleared.

Rim Repair

Bent rim straightening, curb rash repair, and rim color matching. Restore your wheels instead of replacing them at a fraction of the cost.

24/7 Roadside Assistance

Flat on the highway at 2 AM? AJ offers 24-hour roadside assistance including jumpstarts, lockouts, and gas delivery.

Headlight & Bumper

Headlight restoration to remove haze and yellowing. Minor bumper damage repair. Additional services beyond tires to keep your vehicle road-ready.

About AJ

The Tire Shop That Comes to You

15+ Years Behind the Tire Iron

Adrian Johnson (AJ) spent over 15 years in tire sales and service before he saw the gap: people needed tire work done, but nobody wanted to sit in a tire shop waiting room for half a day. So in 2014, he launched InTire Mobile Tire Shop and brought the entire operation to the customer.

Today, AJ rolls up to your driveway, office parking lot, or roadside with everything needed to install, repair, balance, and even fix your rims on the spot. He carries new tires from economy brands to premium names, all at wholesale pricing. And if you get a flat at 2 AM, his 24-hour roadside assistance has you covered.

He is recognized as one of the top tire shops in the state for low pricing and customer treatment. His A+ BBB rating and perfect Yelp score back that up.
