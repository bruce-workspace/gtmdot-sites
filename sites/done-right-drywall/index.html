What We Do

Two Services. Done Right.

We don't do everything. We do drywall and painting — and the water-damage and cabinet work where those two skills overlap. Focus is the quality signal.

Drywall Repair

Holes, cracks, anchor pullouts, seam failures, corner bead damage. Clean cut, proper backing, three-coat finish, texture match.

Drywall Installation

New walls, ceilings, closets, renovations. Full sheet, taped, mudded, sanded, primed. Finished for paint, not just "done."

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors. Single rooms or whole-house repaints. Clean lines, clean protection, clean cleanup.

Cabinet Painting

Kitchen and bath cabinets — proper prep, sand, spray or brush for a factory-finish result. Updates the room without a full remodel.

Water Damage Repair

Leak blown out a ceiling? Pipe failure behind drywall? We handle the patch and paint after remediation — the natural dual-trade job.

Textured Ceiling Repair

Popcorn, orange peel, knockdown — repair, remove, or match existing texture. The ceiling work nobody else wants to do.

Our Story

Two Things. Two Things Only.

David Neel, Owner

Done Right Drywall Repair & Painting was founded in early 2020 with a deliberately narrow scope: drywall and painting. No flooring, no plumbing, no full remodels. Two trades, taught together to every crew member, done on the same visit.

That focus matters because drywall and paint are the trades most often split across two vendors — which means scheduling lags, color mismatches, and texture mismatches. When you hire us, the patch is primed and painted before we leave. No "we'll send the painter Friday."

Five years later, we run two locations (Atlanta and Norcross), carry a 4.8 HomeAdvisor rating, and still refuse to scope-creep. Focus is the quality signal.
