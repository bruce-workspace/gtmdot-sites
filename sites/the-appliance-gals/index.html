What We Fix

Every Appliance in Your Home

Washers, dryers, refrigerators, ovens, dishwashers, and the rest. All major brands. Certified technicians. Honest quotes that often come in under the original estimate.

Washer Repair

Leaking, spinning issues, error codes, not draining. Front-load and top-load. Quick diagnosis, transparent pricing.

Dryer Repair

Not heating, taking forever, making noise, or error codes. Heating elements, belts, motors, and vent issues. Gas and electric.

Refrigerator

Not cooling, ice maker problems, water leaks, temperature control issues. Standard units, French doors, and built-ins.

Oven & Stove

Burners not lighting, uneven heating, temperature sensor issues, self-clean lock problems. Gas, electric, and induction.

Dishwasher

Not draining, not cleaning, error codes, door seal issues. Every major brand. Often cheaper to repair than replace.

Microwave

No heat, sparking, turntable issues, control panel failures. Countertop, over-the-range, and built-in models.

Water Heater

No hot water, leaks, pilot light issues, sediment buildup. Gas and electric tanks plus tankless systems.

Ice Maker & More

Ice makers, garbage disposals, cooktops, and grills. If it's an appliance and it's broken, call the gals.

About the Gals

Sharonda & Eunice

Fifteen Years. Two Women. One Trade.

Sharonda Stewart and Eunice started The Appliance Gals in 2011 with a simple thesis: appliance repair didn't need to feel intimidating, expensive, or pushy. They certified their technicians through NASTeC. They got EPA certified for refrigerant work. And they built a reputation for quoting fairly and often coming in under the original estimate.

Sharonda runs the company as CEO. Eunice runs dispatch and scheduling, and customers mention her by name in their reviews. Between them they've built the kind of business where a customer calls on a Friday evening and gets service on Saturday, at no upcharge. Where the quote you get is usually what you pay, or less.

That's the "T.A.G. we're it" ethos. Professional, certified, flexible, honest. Fifteen years later, it still works.
