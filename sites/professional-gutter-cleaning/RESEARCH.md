## Services Offered

### Core Services
1. **Gutter Cleaning** -- Full debris removal from roof and gutters; rope-and-harness system (no ladders denting gutters); downspout check and flush
2. **Gutter Repair** -- Fix leaks, reattach loose sections, resolve drainage issues
3. **Gutter Installation / Replacement** -- New gutter systems for homes and businesses
4. **Gutter Guards** -- Installation of gutter protection systems
5. **Roofing Repairs** -- Weather damage, shingle replacement, flashing and vent boot repairs

### Additional Services
6. **Tree Pruning** -- Trimming branches near gutters and rooflines
7. **Window Cleaning**
8. **Christmas Light Installation**
9. **Dryer Duct Cleaning**
10. **Fascia, Soffit & Gutter Washing**
11. **Roof Cleaning**

### Service Approach
- 3-person professional crew per job
- Work from the roof using rope-and-harness system (avoids ladder damage to gutters)
- Clean 100% of debris from roof and gutters
- Check and flush every downspout for free flow
- Rinse and clean all debris from bushes, walkways, patios, and driveways below
- Blow off walkways, patios, and driveways after cleanup
- Recommend annual gutter maintenance
