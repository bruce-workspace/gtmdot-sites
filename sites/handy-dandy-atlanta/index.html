What I Do

One Call. Most Things Fixed.

The reason people hire handymen is they don't want to call a plumber, an electrician, a carpenter, and a painter for four different small jobs. I handle all of it.

Drywall Repair

Holes, cracks, water damage, full sheet replacement. Patched, textured, and ready to paint.

Plumbing

Burst pipes, dripping faucets, running toilets, fixture replacements. I've handled emergency plumbing calls for happy customers.

Electrical & Lighting

Outlets, switches, light fixtures, ceiling fans, and basic electrical repairs. Safe, code-aware work.

Deck Renovation

Board replacement, railing repair, staining, and full deck refresh. Make outdoor living usable again.

Interior Repairs

Doors, trim, cabinets, hardware, ceiling fans, closet systems, and the list of things that get tagged "one day I'll fix that."

Mailbox & Exterior

Mailbox installation and repair, exterior trim, fence fixes, and general outdoor maintenance.

Painting

Interior painting, touch-ups, trim work, and small rooms. Clean lines, solid coverage, dried and ready.

General Handyman

If your to-do list has small jobs that don't fit any single trade, that's what I'm for. One call, one person, work done.

About Ruslan

One Guy Who Shows Up

Russ KH, Handy Dandy Atlanta

Ruslan (Russ) has been running Handy Dandy Atlanta for over five years. No crew, no subcontractors. When you book him, he shows up and does the work himself.

The range is what makes him useful. Most homeowners have a list: a leaky faucet, a hole in the drywall from moving, a ceiling fan that needs replacing, a deck board that's rotted, a mailbox that's leaning. Calling four different trades for that list means four service calls, four quotes, four coordination headaches. Ruslan takes the whole list.

His work philosophy, in his own words: "I approach every task with enthusiasm and look to exceed client expectations. From small jobs to large jobs. I have experience and skills to get your job done right."
