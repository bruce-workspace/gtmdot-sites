## Company Story
Cleveland Electric is a **fifth-generation family business** founded in **1925** by Ras Hitson Cleveland when he acquired Browning Electrical Motor Repair Company after his father's passing. What began as an electric motor repair company has evolved over nearly a century into one of Atlanta's leading specialty contractors.

**Key Milestones:**
- **1925:** Ras Hitson Cleveland founds the company
- **1959:** Founder passes operations to sons Louie and James
- **1960s-1970s:** Louie continues electrical repair; James launches specialty contracting
- **1979:** Awarded 10-year, $315M contract for Vogtle Nuclear Plant (first two units)
- **1980s-1990s:** Major automotive manufacturing (GM facilities, Ford Hapeville plant)
- **1998:** Sold motor repair business to focus on specialty contracting
- **Today:** Fifth generation of family leadership
