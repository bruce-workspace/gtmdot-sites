What We Do

Tires, Fast and Fair

New tires, used tires, flat repair, and towing. A small shop doing the work the chains overcharge for, with the diagnostic honesty the chains don't offer.

New Tires

All sizes, most major brands, install included. We'll quote you a real number — multiple customers tell us we came in lower than the chains.

Used Tires

Quality used tires at a fraction of new. Great for older vehicles, spare upgrades, or getting you home after a blowout without breaking the month.

Flat Repair

Plug, patch, or full repair. Andrew fixes tires other shops told you to replace — customers come in with that exact story and leave with a repaired tire.

Installation + Balance

Install, mount, balance. Quick turnaround — Saturday-evening flat repair stories are in the reviews section below.

Towing

Tow-in service so you don't have to figure out how to get a blown tire to the shop. Call us and we'll sort it.

Honest Diagnosis

If your tire doesn't need replacing, we'll tell you. If the wear is uneven because of alignment, we'll say so. Diagnosis first, sale second.
