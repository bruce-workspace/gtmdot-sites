What We Do

Concrete & Decorative Work

From a simple poured slab to a stamped concrete patio, Hugo and his crew handle every concrete job with the same fast turnaround and daily communication customers keep mentioning.

Concrete Driveways

New pours, driveway extensions and additions, and complete replacements. One-day completion on most driveways. Level, even, and long-lasting.

Patios & Slabs

Concrete patios, pool decks, carport pads, and general flatwork. Custom shapes and finishes to match your property.

Stamped & Decorative

Stamped concrete patterns (slate, brick, tile), colored concrete, and exposed aggregate finishes. The look of stone at the cost of concrete.

Concrete Repair & Leveling

Cracked driveways, settled slabs, uneven surfaces. Proper leveling and repair that addresses the underlying issue, not just the surface.

Walkways & Steps

Sidewalks, walkways, front steps, and stone setting. Safe, level, and properly drained for Atlanta's wet seasons.

Emergency Concrete

Cracked foundations, broken walkways, or safety-critical concrete damage. Emergency response for situations that can't wait for next week.

Concrete, Done Right and On Time

Hugo Tamayo founded Doctor Concrete ATL in March 2019. Seven years later, the company has earned a perfect 5.0 rating on Angi with a reputation built on two specific things customers keep calling out: fast turnaround (quote-to-pour in two days is common) and daily communication (no ghosting, clear arrival times, clear scope).
