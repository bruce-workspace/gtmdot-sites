What We Clean

Fabric, Carpet & Upholstery

Hot water extraction and steam cleaning for every fabric surface in your home. Reuben brings the equipment to you and does the work himself.

Carpet Steam Cleaning

Wall-to-wall carpet cleaning using hot water extraction. Lifts dirt and allergens from deep in the fibers, leaving carpets fresh and dry faster than traditional shampooing.

Upholstery Cleaning

Couches, sectionals, loveseats, chairs. Toddler residue, pet stains, and years of wear steam-cleaned out. One reviewer had a secondhand couch sanitized and was thrilled.

Area Rugs

On-site area rug cleaning at your home. Oriental, wool, synthetic, shag. Reuben cleaned rugs for one customer during their move-in.

Pet Stain & Odor

Pet accidents, marking, and deep-set odor removal. Not just surface masking — we treat the source so the smell doesn't come back.

Mattress Cleaning

Deep steam cleaning for mattresses to eliminate dust mites, allergens, and stains. You spend a third of your life on it — worth keeping clean.

Tile & Grout

Deep cleaning for tile floors and grout lines. Lift years of grime and discoloration that mopping can't reach.

About Reuben

The Guy Who Shows Up and Does the Work

No Crews. No Subcontractors. Just Reuben.

Dream Steam Fabric Cleaners is owner-operated. Every job you book is Reuben himself showing up, explaining what he's going to do, and doing it. That's why his Google reviews are 5.0 across the board — when the owner does the work, the standard stays consistent.

Reuben works mobile, bringing the equipment to your home. He services carpet, upholstery, area rugs, mattresses, tile, and grout. He'll tell you honestly what's realistic to remove (some stains are permanent — he'd rather set expectations than oversell) and then he gets to work.

One customer booked him for a Sunday appointment at their new house. Reuben showed up on time, and when the homeowner got injured mid-move, he stopped his work to help finish unloading the truck. That's the kind of guy he is.
