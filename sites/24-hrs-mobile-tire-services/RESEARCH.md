## Site Build Notes

- **Brand tone:** Fast, owner-operated, "we come to you, 24/7" — not a fleet/franchise tone.
- **Brand color:** Suggest safety orange (#FF6A00) or high-visibility red-orange. Ties to roadside/safety imagery and stands out on a dark theme. Alternate: electric yellow (#FFD400) if a "tow-truck / beacon" vibe is preferred.
- **Hero:** Mobile tire service van or operator changing a tire roadside at night — must show the service happening at the customer's location, not inside a shop.
- **Stats bar (suggested, all grounded):**
  - 24/7 Availability
  - 5.0 Star Rating
  - 100% 5-Star Reviews
  - Midtown-Based, Metro-Wide Coverage
  (Avoid "X years in business" — not verified.)
- **FAQ angles (specific to this operator):**
  - How fast do you arrive? (reviews emphasize speed)
  - Do you bring new tires, or just repair mine?
  - Do you work on I-75 / I-285 / GA-400?
  - Do you service after midnight?
  - Do you take card on-site?
  - Can you install run-flats / TPMS-equipped wheels?
  - What if my spare is also flat?
  - Do you service commercial vehicles / box trucks?
- **Form fields:** Name, phone, current location (address or "on [highway/exit]"), vehicle year/make/model, tire size (if known), issue (flat / blowout / need new tires / rotation / alignment), optional photo upload of the tire.
- **Claim bar hook:** "Stranded? Tap to call — answered 24/7."
- **Exit popup:** "Still have a flat? Save this number." + one-tap dial.
