What We Do

Complete Pool Care

From weekly maintenance to rescuing a pool that's gone completely green, Chris and his team handle every aspect of residential pool service.

Weekly Pool Maintenance

Regular cleaning, chemical balancing, and water testing. Flexible scheduling matched to your pool's needs and your budget. Year-round service available.

Green-to-Clean Recovery

Neglected pool turned into a swamp? This is Chris's specialty. Multiple customers describe dark green lagoons transformed into crystal clear water in days.

Pool Opening

Cover removal, thorough cleaning, water balancing, and full equipment inspection. Pump, filter, and plumbing checked and repaired or upgraded as needed.

Pool Closing & Winterization

Seasonal closing tailored to your specific pool. Proper winterization protects your equipment and makes spring opening smooth and fast.

Equipment Repair

Pool pumps, filters, plumbing, and heating systems diagnosed and repaired. Chris saved one customer from replacing an entire pump by finding the real issue.

Water Testing & Inspection

Detailed chemical testing with results in your service report. Pool system inspections for new homeowners, equipment walkthroughs, and maintenance recommendations.

About Chris

Built on Service, Not Contracts

From Layoff to Legacy

When COVID-19 hit in March 2020, Chris Perez was laid off from Delta Airlines. Instead of waiting for the world to reopen, he pivoted. He joined Pool Scouts of North Atlanta as General Manager, managing four territories across the metro. He learned the business inside and out.

Then he went independent. Perez Pools LLC was born from a simple belief: if you care about the service you provide, the business takes care of itself. That's why Chris doesn't require contracts. Every visit earns the next one.

His team sends en-route text messages so you know exactly when they're coming. Every service ends with a detailed report including before-and-after photos and water test results. Background-checked technicians. Always in uniform. It's the level of communication and transparency that made 35 homeowners leave five-star reviews.
