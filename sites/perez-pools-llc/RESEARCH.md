## Business Story
Chris Perez is originally from Florida and has lived in the North Atlanta area for most of his life (Roswell area). He graduated from the University of South Carolina Aiken in spring 2018. He worked briefly at an SEO agency, then joined Delta Airlines in December 2019. He was laid off in March 2020 when COVID-19 hit. Through a connection from a prior job, he landed a position as General Manager at Pool Scouts of North Atlanta (a franchise), where he managed four territories in the Atlanta market starting in 2020.

Chris later launched his own independent company, Perez Pools LLC, which appears to have been formed around 2021 (Thumbtack background check completed December 2021, earliest Thumbtack reviews from late 2021). The company has been operating for approximately 5 years.

His philosophy: "All pool skills are learnable, but what it comes down to is providing the service and caring about the service you provide." and "Not everyone cares for our customers as much as we do."

**Key differentiators:**
- No contracts -- earns business with each service

- Enhanced communications: en-route text messages, post-service reports with before/after photos, water test results, and service details
- 3 employees
- 22+ jobs completed on Thumbtack alone

## Services
1. **Pool Maintenance & Cleaning** -- Weekly/regular pool cleaning, chemical balancing, flexible scheduling to match pool needs and budget, year-round service
2. **Pool Opening** -- Cover removal, cleaning and balancing water, equipment inspection (pump, filter, plumbing), necessary repairs or upgrades
3. **Pool Closing & Winterization** -- Seasonal closing, winterization tailored to specific pool needs
4. **Green-to-Clean Recovery** -- Specialty in transforming neglected, green/algae-filled pools back to swim-ready condition
5. **Equipment Repair & Service** -- Pool pump repair/replacement, filter installation/replacement, plumbing checks, equipment efficiency optimization
6. **Pool Inspection** -- Pool system inspections for new homeowners, equipment walkthroughs
7. **Water Testing** -- Chemical testing with detailed results in service reports

**Pool types served:** In-ground and above-ground residential pools
**Pool surfaces:** Concrete, tile, fiberglass, vinyl
**Water types:** Chlorinated and saltwater
**Additional features serviced:** Heating systems, cleaning systems, covers, lighting, decks, diving boards/slides

**Note:** Does NOT appear to offer new pool construction, resurfacing, or major renovation services. Focus is maintenance, cleaning, repair, and seasonal services.
