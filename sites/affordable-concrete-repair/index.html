What We Do

Concrete & Masonry Services

From new pours to repairs other contractors left behind. Maurice and his crew handle every type of concrete and masonry work for residential and commercial properties.

Driveways & Slabs

New concrete driveways, carport pads, and foundation slabs. Poured and finished to last decades in Florida's climate.

Patios & Porches

Screened-in porches, back patios, and outdoor living areas. Custom shapes and finishes to match your home.

Sidewalks & Walkways

Safe, level walkways that drain properly. From residential paths to commercial sidewalks and ADA-compliant approaches.

Concrete Repair

Pool deck resurfacing, patio refinishing, crack repair, and spraycrete application. Fixing what others left behind is a specialty.

Masonry & Block Work

Block walls, brick work, stone veneer, and retaining walls. Structural and decorative masonry for any property.

Stamped & Decorative

Stamped concrete and spraycrete finishes that look like stone, brick, or tile at a fraction of the cost.

Pool Deck Repair

Resurfacing, leveling, and refinishing pool decks. Non-slip finishes that look great and hold up to Florida sun and water.

Commercial Concrete

Parking pads, commercial foundations, warehouse floors, and structural concrete for business properties.
