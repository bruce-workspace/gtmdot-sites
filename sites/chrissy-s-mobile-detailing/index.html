What We Do

Detail, Correct, Coat, Tint

Five signature services covering everything from a basic exterior wash to full ceramic protection. All mobile, all at your location.

Exterior Detail

Hand wash, clay bar, wax, tire shine, glass cleaning. Brings back the shine without the swirl marks that come from automated wash tunnels.

Interior Detail

Seats, carpets, dash, console, headliner. Stains removed, pet hair extracted, kids-van chaos erased. "Better than new" is a direct quote from a reviewer.

Clay & Seal

Multi-step paint decontamination plus a sealant layer. Removes bonded contaminants (iron, tree sap, industrial fallout) and leaves a slick, hydrophobic finish.

Paint Correction & Ceramic

Multi-stage polish to remove swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation, followed by professional ceramic coating. Years of paint protection, not months.

Ceramic Window Tint

Legal automotive window tint with ceramic film. Better heat rejection than dyed or metallic tint, doesn't interfere with signals, longer color stability.

Stain & Odor Removal

Chemical stains, pet accidents, vomit, smoke, and coffee. Specializing in the messes that other detailers write off as permanent.

About Chrissy

Miami Roots, Atlanta Service

Detail That's Actually Personal

Chrissy runs a Black-owned, women-owned mobile detailing operation in Atlanta. Before Chrissy's Mobile Detailing, the business operated as Miami Style Detail — a nod to the standards of the South Beach luxury market where Chrissy learned the craft.

What makes the business different isn't the equipment. It's the attention to the details other detailers skip. Services cover everything from an exterior wash to multi-stage paint correction with ceramic coating, plus legal ceramic window tint. All mobile. All at your driveway or garage.
