## Site Build Notes (for GTMDot builder)
- **Brand color suggestion:** Deep teal or chrome-blue accent over dark theme. Detailing sector likes high-contrast chrome/sapphire. Alternatively: champagne gold (ties to "Miami style" heritage and Black/women-owned premium positioning). Recommend: `--brand: #00C2D1` (electric aqua) for energy matching Miami aesthetic, OR `--brand: #D4AF37` (champagne gold).
- **Hero shot:** Need a real before/after or glossy paint-reflection shot. Ceramic coating water-beading shot is a strong alternative.
- **Stats bar ideas (all defensible):**
  - 5.0 Google rating
  - 100+ reviews
  - 10+ cities served
  - Black & Women Owned
- **FAQ must-haves specific to this business:**
  - Do you come to me? (Yes — fully mobile.)
  - What areas do you serve? (List the 10 cities.)
  - How long does ceramic coating take?
  - Can you remove heavy interior stains / pet hair / smells? (Reviews explicitly cite this.)
  - Is ceramic tint legal in Georgia? (Relevant for tint service.)
  - How far in advance should I book?
- **Form fields:** Name, phone, email, vehicle make/model/year, service selection (exterior / interior / ceramic / tint / package), photo upload (strongly encouraged — condition assessment), service address/zip, preferred date.
- **Icon mapping (cross-check ICON-MAPPING.md at build time):**
  - Mobile service → `truck` or `car`
  - Exterior detail → `sparkles` or `spray-can`
  - Interior detail → `vacuum` or `armchair` (check mapping)
  - Ceramic coating → `shield-check` or `droplets`
  - Paint correction → `paintbrush` or `wand-sparkles`
  - Window tint → `square-dashed` or `sun-dim`
- **Trust signals for marquee:** Black Owned, Women Owned, Mobile Service, Ceramic Certified, 5-Star Rated, Fully Insured (verify before using), Serving Atlanta Metro
