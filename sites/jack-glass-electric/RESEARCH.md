Jack C. Glass Electric is a **third-generation family business** founded in 1970 by Jack C. Glass. The company is now owned and run by his grandsons **Jimmy and Kerry Glass** (James A. Glass listed as President on BBB). What started as a small family electrical service has grown into a trusted name across the Atlanta metro area over 56 years. The company prides itself on family values, honest pricing, and showing up when they say they will.
