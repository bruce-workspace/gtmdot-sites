Our Story

Three Generations. One Standard.

When Jack C. Glass started his electrical business in 1970, he had a simple promise: show up on time, do honest work, and charge a fair price. Fifty-six years later, his grandsons Jimmy and Kerry Glass are running the family business with the same values that built it.

What started as a one-man operation in Mableton has grown into one of the most trusted electrical service companies in the Atlanta metro area. Today, the Glass Electric team handles everything from simple outlet repairs to full-home rewiring, always treating every customer's home like their own.

"Three generations of the Glass family, one unbroken standard of quality."

1970
Jack C. Glass founds Glass Electric in Mableton, GA, serving residential customers across the Atlanta area.
1987
The company incorporates as Jack C. Glass Electric Co., Inc., expanding into commercial electrical services.
2000s
Second generation takes the reins, growing the company's reputation across Fulton County and beyond.
Today
Grandsons Jimmy and Kerry Glass lead the third generation with 85+ five-star reviews and an A+ BBB rating.
