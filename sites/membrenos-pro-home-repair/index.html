About Hector

Precision, Passion, Integrity

The Pro Who Does It All

Hector Membreno founded Membreno's Pro Home Repair in 2018 with a clear mission: deliver excellent workmanship and complete customer satisfaction from start to completion. Eight years later, that mission has earned him 69 Google reviews and a reputation across Gwinnett County as the contractor who shows up early, works fast, and leaves the site cleaner than he found it.

What sets Hector apart is range. Most contractors specialize in one trade. Hector's crew handles painting, electrical, plumbing, decks, drywall, backsplash, cabinets, and full remodels. One customer had painting, backsplash, 21 cabinet handles, and electrical panel work all completed in a single 7-hour visit. That's efficiency you don't find with a general handyman.

Hector speaks both English and Spanish, serving Gwinnett County's diverse community. He's available Monday through Saturday, 7 AM to 6 PM, and takes calls on Sundays for urgent requests.
