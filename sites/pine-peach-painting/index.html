What We Do

Painting & Beyond

From a single accent wall to a full exterior transformation, Fernando and his team handle every project with meticulous care and craftsmanship.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, and detail work. We prep thoroughly, protect your furniture, and leave every room looking brand new.

Exterior Painting

Complete exterior prep, prime, and paint. We handle siding, trim, shutters, porches, and everything weather touches.

Cabinet Painting

Kitchen and bathroom cabinet transformations. Professional prep and finish that makes old cabinets look factory-fresh.

Deck Staining

Restore and protect your deck with professional staining and sealing. We clean, sand, and apply premium finishes.

Drywall Repair

Holes, cracks, water damage, and texture matching. We fix the wall before we paint it so the finish is flawless.

Color Consultation

Not sure which colors to pick? Fernando helps you choose palettes that complement your home's style and natural light.

Pressure Washing

Driveways, patios, siding, and decks. We blast away years of grime before painting or as a standalone service.

Carpentry & Shiplap

Custom bookshelves, shiplap accent walls, rotted wood repair, and trim work. Fernando builds it and paints it.

Meet Fernando

A Family Craft, Passed Down

When Fernando Chamorro started Pine Peach Painting in 2009, he brought something rare to the Atlanta painting scene: genuine care for every wall he touched. Over 17 years, that care has earned him a 4.8-star reputation and a loyal base of repeat customers across Sandy Springs, Brookhaven, and greater Atlanta.

Today, Fernando works alongside his son, who is learning the craft from the ground up. Together with a senior assistant known for "impeccable trim work," the Pine Peach team treats every project like it's their own home.

What sets Pine Peach apart isn't just painting. Fernando builds bookshelves, installs shiplap, repairs drywall, and restores decks. When homeowners call Fernando, they get a complete home transformation partner, not just a painter with a roller.

A standard bedroom typically runs $300-$600 depending on size, ceiling height, and prep work needed. Larger rooms, vaulted ceilings, and rooms requiring extensive patching or priming will be on the higher end. We provide a detailed, no-obligation estimate before starting.
Yes, cabinet painting is one of our specialties. A full kitchen typically takes 3-5 days depending on the number of cabinets and the finish you want. We remove doors, sand, prime, and apply multiple coats for a factory-quality finish that lasts.
We handle everything. Our team moves furniture to the center of the room or out entirely, lays down protective drop cloths, and puts everything back when we're done. You don't need to lift a finger.
Absolutely. Drywall repair is part of our standard prep process. We patch holes, fill cracks, skim-coat rough areas, and match existing textures before applying any paint. The result is a smooth, flawless finish.
