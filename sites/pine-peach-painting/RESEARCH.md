## Company Story
Pine Peach Painting was established in 2009 by **Fernando Chamorro**, who has over 10 years of experience (now 17+ years) in the painting industry. Fernando runs the business with his son, who is being trained in the craft, making it a **family operation**. The company brings fresh perspectives and inviting finishes to homes throughout Atlanta, combining skilled craftsmanship with a client-focused approach. They specialize in both interior and exterior painting with additional carpentry and repair capabilities.
