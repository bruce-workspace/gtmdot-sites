What We Do

Residential Electrical, Full Scope

From a single outlet to a whole-house panel. Licensed electricians doing the everyday work and the high-end wiring most shops won't touch.

Panel Updates

Service upgrades, sub-panels, fuse box replacements. Bring older Atlanta homes up to current code and current load.

Home Theater Wiring

Media rooms, in-wall speakers, projector power, low-voltage control. Clean installs that look built-in, not bolted-on.

Security + Motion Lighting

Motion-activated exterior lighting, dusk-to-dawn fixtures, smart switches. Safety and visibility without floodlight overkill.

Smoke + CO Detectors

Hardwired smoke and carbon monoxide detection. Code-compliant interconnected systems on new construction or retrofit.

Kitchen + Laundry Circuits

Dedicated circuits for appliances, under-cabinet lighting, pendant fixtures, GFCI countertop outlets — the kitchen work that shows up in every remodel.

Basement + Media Wiring

Basement finishes, media rooms, home offices. Full wiring, cable management, lighting layouts that actually suit the room.

Outdoor + Landscape

Patio lighting, landscape uplighting, path fixtures, exterior outlets. Curbside polish and functional outdoor living.

Garage + Home Office

Garage door opener circuits, workbench lighting, home office cabling, printer drops, network runs. The work most electricians rush.

Our Story

Two Generations, One Phone

Why "& Sons" Is in the Name

Harrison & Sons Electrical opened in Atlanta in 2005. The name was a commitment, not a branding decision — when the Harrison sons were old enough, they joined the business. Two decades later, the same family still runs every job.

That matters for an electrician more than most trades. Panel work, home theater wiring, and lighting design require experience and judgment that don't come from a year-long apprenticeship. When you call us, the person on the other end has done thousands of Atlanta homes — and remembers yours next time you call.

From full panel upgrades on older homes along Cascade Road to media room installs in Brookhaven, the Harrisons cover the scope most electricians split across specialists. One team, one family, one phone.

2005
Harrison & Sons Electrical Service LLC founded in Atlanta. Licensed residential electrician serving southwest Atlanta and metro.
2010s
Home theater and media-room wiring added as premium residential niche. Security lighting and safety (smoke/CO) become core offerings.
2020s
Directory reviews cross the 100-mark. Sons fully integrated into the operation — the "& Sons" in the name is now literal across all jobs.
Today
20+ years, 4.8 stars, full residential scope including panels, theater, outdoor, kitchen, bathroom, garage, and home office.
